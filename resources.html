Islamic Resources

Online Libraries Educational Websites Community Organizations Mobile Apps

Online Libraries

  • Quran - A comprehensive online resource for the Quran, including translations and Tafsir.
  • Sunnah - A digital library of Hadith collections from various sources.
  • Al-Islam- A vast collection of Islamic texts, including books and articles.

Educational Websites

  • Islamic Studies - Offers educational material and courses on various aspects of Islam.
  • Khan Academy - Provides free educational resources that include Islamic history and culture sections.
  • Coursera - Offers courses on Islamic studies and related subjects.

Community Organizations

  • Islamic Circle of North America (ICNA) - Provides community support and Islamic resources in North America.
  • Muslim Aid - A global humanitarian organization providing aid and development services.
  • Islamic Relief Worldwide- Offers humanitarian relief and development programs globally.

Mobile Apps

  • Quran Majeed - A popular app for reading and listening to the Quran with translations.
  • Muslim Pro - Provides prayer times, Quran readings, and Islamic content.
  • Islampedia - Offers a range of Islamic knowledge and information.
